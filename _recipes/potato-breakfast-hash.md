## Ingredients

- 6-8 gold potatoes (*NOTE: may be subbed for sweet potatoes also*)
- 1 bell pepper
- 6 strips of bacon
- 1 onion
- 6 eggs

#### Seasonings

- salt & pepper
- onion powder
- garlic powder
- adobo goya

## Recipe

1. Add bacon to a cold pan and cook until crispy. you can either cut it into
   pieces before you cook it or crumble it at the end, up to you.
2. While bacon is cooking, dice your potatoes, bell pepper, and onion.
3. Once the bacon is cooked, remove from pan and add potatoes directly into the
   same pan with the rendered bacon fat. Cook for 10-15 minutes, then add the
   peppers and onions along with seasonings.
4. once everything is cooked and has nice coloring, scramble 6 eggs and add
   them to the pan.
5. Once everything is cooked, serve and enjoy!
