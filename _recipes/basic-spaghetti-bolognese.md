## Ingredients

- 1 lb pasta (any kind)
- 1 jar tomato sauce
- 1 lb ground beef

#### Seasonings
- salt & pepper
- garlic powder
- onion powder
- oregano

## Recipe

1. Add seasonings to a pot containing the tomato sauce and ground beef.
2. Boil Water with a ton of salt in it, then add pasta once water is boiling.
3. Once the ground beef is cooked, about an our but could be left longer,
   combine with the cooked pasta.
4. Enjoy!
