## Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 red onion
- 1 can black beans
- 1 can corn
- 1 can fire-roasted tomatoes w/ green chilies
- 8 oz cream cheese (can sub neufchatel)
- 4 cups chicken broth

#### Seasonings

- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 pouch ranch powder

## Recipe

1. Combine all ingredients and seasonings **_except_** the cream cheese in a crockpot and leave on low for 6 hours.
2. Add cream cheese (preferably softnened) to a bowl with a few ladels of the soup and mix to combine before adding the mixture back to the crockpot with the rest of the soup. A hand mixer works best.
3. Serve with shredded cheese and avocado!
